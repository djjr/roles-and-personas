[K. Eric Drexler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K._Eric_Drexler) is a researcher associated with the Future of Humanity Institute (Oxford), best known for foundational work in nanotechnology (*Engines of Creation*, 1986) and for *Reframing Superintelligence* (FHI Technical Report, 2019), which introduced the **Open Cog** / **Open Agency** framework for thinking about advanced AI capabilities in non-agentic terms.

In this wiki, Drexler is relevant primarily as the author of the [[sources/role-architectures-drexler|Role Architectures]] post (LessWrong, ~2023), which applies his non-agentic LLM thesis to practical deployment via [[definitions/role-architecture|role architectures]] and [[definitions/role-fidelity|role fidelity]]. His broader *Reframing Superintelligence* is a cited background source in the Role Architectures post and is a candidate for future ingestion.
